Introduction

Welcome to Blowfish Advanced CS, the software for protecting your private files on your Windows PC and Network.

Blowfish Advanced CS is a file encryption program, protecting your files with a key built from a password or a key disk, so that noone but you has access to the contents. Blowfish Advanced CS wipes sensitive files that are no longer needed, so nobody will be able to restore them. Working with encrypted files and clearing empty disk space are other useful features.

Today we are in the information age and encrypting data becomes more and more important for most of us. There are many reasons why data have to be protected from unauthorized access, e.g. sensitive medical data, private or business documents or just some hot stuff from the Internet.

There are many ways to make data readable only to selected people. Besides physical measures like locking removable disks into a safe, or cheap solutions like hiding files with steganography, the only way to make files really unaccessible is to use strong cryptography. That means high-end encryption algorithms with keys long enough to resist any attacks, combined with secure removal of the original data.

That's what Blowfish Advanced CS does, bundled with a richly featured user interface for the daily work, powered by an universal crypto engine, well integrated in the Windows environment and able to automate most of the handling.

Equipped with the most proven and modern security technologies, capable of encrypting and wiping even the largest number of files with just a few mouse clicks, incredibly fast and powerful to use - Blowfish Advanced CS is one of the top software encryption products available today.
