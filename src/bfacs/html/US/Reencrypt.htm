Reencrypt

Reencryption means that you are able to change the key and the encryption algorithm of encrypted files with one simple action.

Using the conventional way you will have to decrypt the files and encrypt them again, changing the algorithms between those actions. With reencryption you just provide the old encryption key and algorithm and then declare a new key and encryption algorithm immediately.

The process keeps everything of the selected cryptfiles (including their names), the data is not even uncompressed, but just moved to the new files without any changes. This leads to a very good performance and increases security.
