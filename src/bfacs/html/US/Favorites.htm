Favorites

By the same way you bookmark interesting links in your Internet browser you can create a list of your favorite paths in Blowfish Advanced CS.

If you want to store the current path as a favorite, just click on the menu option Favorites...Add Current Path. The path name will then be permanently available in the Favorites menu list. Changing to completely different paths is now just a matter of a second without browsing down, up or cross with far too much trouble.

You can edit your favorites by clicking on the Favorites...Organize... menu item. In the appearing dialog you can remove old entries from the list. Multi selections are also possible, if you want to remove more than one favorite path at once.
