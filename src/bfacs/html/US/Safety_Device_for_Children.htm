Safety Device for Children

By the same way you keep your car doors secured against the danger of being opened by children you can protect Blowfish Advanced CS being used from persons not knowing what they're doing.

As you know the software is able destroy huge amounts of data within seconds. E.g. if a child is playing with your computer and discovers Blowfish Advanced CS it might be possible that files are encrypted or (more possible) wiped without any chances for recovery.

By setting a simple password, which must be entered every time when the program is started, you can avoid this danger in most of the such cases. Remember that this is not a protection against any other intruders with bad intentions, as the password is stored in the configuration file. If someone can launch applications on your PC without your knowledge it is easy to destroy data, even without Blowfish Advanced CS.

You can turn off the safety device by entering an empty password (not during the startup, of course).
